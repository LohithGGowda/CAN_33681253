What is Infertility Treatment?

IVF vs. DI vs. ICSI

Understanding the differences between these fertility treatments can help individuals make informed decisions.

IVF (In Vitro Fertilization)

A process where eggs are fertilized outside the body and then implanted.

DI (Donor Insemination)

Sperm from a donor is used for conception without IVF.

ICSI (Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection)

A specialized form of IVF where a single sperm is injected directly into an egg.

Benefits of Infertility Treatment

  • Increased chances of conception
  • Personalized treatment plans using AI
  • Advanced reproductive technology
  • Options for individuals with complex fertility issues
